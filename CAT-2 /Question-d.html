Arc Shot


A shot in which the sublject is photographed by an encircling or moving camera


Interlude

A brief, intervening film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film


Prologue


A speech, preface, introducing, or brief scene preceding the the main action or plot od a film; contrast to epilogue
